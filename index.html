In today's time, Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) has become one of the most concerning lifestyle diseases which has affected over 7.8 million people in India. But with the advancement of science and technology, it has become possible to live a completely normal life with a few lifestyle changes. If you have CKD, your kidneys can't filter blood as well as they should, and this can lead to other health problems, such as heart disease and stroke. While it is not possible to reverse kidney damage, you can take steps to slow it down and Dialysis is the way to go about it. Dialysis is a procedure of removal of toxic wastes and fluid from the blood when the kidneys stop functioning properly. It is actually a great support system which helps making life normal for those suffering from end stage renal failure. At Takshshila, we provide In-Centre Hemodialysis meaning the dialysis is provided at our facility center.
